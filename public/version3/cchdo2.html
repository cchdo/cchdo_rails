Welcome to the CCHDO

The CCHDO's primary mission is to be a repository and distribution center for CTD and Hydrographic data sets of the highest possible quality. These data are a product of WOCE, CLIVAR and numerous other oceanographic research programs -- past, present and to come.

Whenever possible we provide these data in three widely-used formats: WOCE, netCDF and WHP-Exchange, which we recommend for data submissions to the CCHDO.

This site is funded by the National Science Foundation.
